Football Frenzy App is an innovative and engaging fundraising management platform for organizations like yours!

Football Frenzy App provides non-profit organizations with customer and ticket management software for their NFL based fundraising programs. Our user-friendly platform streamlines all the administrative work, allowing organizations to focus on their fundraising goals while maintaining full control over donations and prize distribution.

Revenue Generation Model

Investment Structure

Setting an affordable ticket price helps maximize ticket sales potential while keeping participation accessible to a broad audience.

Prize Distribution

A consistent weekly prize structure encourages ongoing participation and excitement throughout the season, supporting sustained engagement and fundraising momentum.

Revenue Allocation

All net proceeds remain with your organization. We do not collect payments or distribute prizes. Your organization retains full control of all funds.

Customizable prize structures available to align with your organization's specific fundraising objectives and engagement goals.

How it works

  • Sign up on the Football Frenzy App, set up your organization profile, and launch your campaign.
  • Set the ticket price.
  • Set the weekly prize distribution: For 1st place, 2nd place, 3rd place, 4th place, and Last place.
  • The program runs for 18 weeks of games, with 32 total teams in play.
  • Each ticket features a unique combination of three teams per week, and the total number of unique tickets created per program is 4,096.
  • The weekly ticket score is calculated by adding the scores of all three teams for that week. View an example ticket.
  • Teams that are on a bye week will be assigned the previous week's score.
  • Share the campaign with your community and encourage participation.
Calculate your potential revenue
