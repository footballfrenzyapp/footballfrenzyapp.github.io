Revenue Generation Model

Investment Structure

Setting an affordable ticket price helps maximize fundraising potential while keeping participation accessible to a broad audience.

Prize Distribution

A consistent weekly prize structure encourages ongoing participation and excitement throughout the season, supporting sustained engagement and fundraising momentum.

Revenue Allocation

All net proceeds remain with your organization.

Customizable prize structures available to align with your organization's specific fundraising objectives and engagement goals.

How it works

  • Sign up with Football Frenzy App and create an organization profile, then create a fundraising campaign.
  • Set ticket price per ticket for $20
  • Set the weekly prize distribution: $250 for 1st place, $100 for 2nd, $75 for 3rd, $50 for 4th, and $25 for 5th. Totaling $500 per week across 18 weeks, for a cumulative seasonal payout of $9,000.
  • Tickets scores are calculated base on that weeks scores each week a ticket has three unique teams.
  • Share the campaign with your community and encourage participation.

Our platform manages ticket creation, tracks ticket sales, and automatically determines weekly winners based on scores. We do not collect payments or distribute prizes. Your organization retains full control of all funds. This is a fundraising management tool designed to streamline operations and support your efforts.
